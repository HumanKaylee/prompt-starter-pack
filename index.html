How To Use It

Open a template, swap in your variables, paste it into your model, then refine with the tuning notes.

1. Pick the closest task instead of starting from a blank chat.
2. Replace the `{{VARIABLES}}` with your real code, logs, or context.
3. Use the worked example as a quality bar before trusting the answer.
4. Read the tuning notes when the task is large, ambiguous, or high-risk.
